Hotels

For all of you who need a place to sleep in Hamburg during The Preface here are some easy options all in walking distance to the betahaus.

St. Pauli Lodge

Located no more than 4 minutes away from the venue (Map) the St. Pauli Lodge offers double and single rooms at a good rate including coffee, tea and wifi. Check their website for all the details

www.sankt-pauli-lodge.de (de)

MyPlace

For those who are looking to stay two nights in Hamburg the MyPlace is a good option. Again you won't walk for more than 5 minutes to the betahaus (Map). More information on the website

www.myplace-hamburg.de

bedpark

If you are on a budget the bedpark has you covered. It takes you a 10 minute walk to the betahaus (Map). Book rooms and get more details on their website: www.bedpark.de (de)
