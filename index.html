Schedule

All talks are in German / Slides will be in English.

Due to it's intimate size The Preface will be a single-track event. Besides lots of insights on the XING platform and reports from our partners there will be plenty of time to talk shop, brain storm and get to know each other.

Great catering, drinks and of course good coffee will guide you through the day.

After lunch we are welcoming you in our Clinic. A place to meet 1:1 with the XING experts. Time to ask your questions, give feedback or tell us about your problems. We will come equipped with laptops, notepads to help you out and listen. And it is not just about technical questions. Our growth hacking experts are happy to give you some hands on advice on how to grow your app or website and gain more customers, too.

Please bare with us in case of last minute schedule changes.

After Party

Of course The Preface is not over when the lights go dark at the betahaus! Once we are done there, feel free to join one of our three different tours of some of Hamburg's most amazing spots. At the end of the tours everyone will come together at the Santa Pauli christmas fair for some Glühwein right on the Reeperbahn.

Please understand that all of the tours are paid for by yourself.

Register for a tour here.

Tour 1: Harbour

Enjoy a boat-tour through the illuminated Speicherstadt, drinks with a breathtaking view over the city followed by St. Pauli's best Burgers at The Bird. (Costs: boat-tour 20€, ordered food & drinks)

Tour 2: St. Pauli

Get a great outlook from Michel's church-tower before having dinner at the popular maritime style restaurant Old Commercial Room. Afterwards the St. Pauli night guard will share his "Seemannsgarn" on a rather uncommon tour around St. Pauli. (Costs: Guide 19€, ordered food & drinks)

Tour 3: Sternschanze

At first the great team of behind the developer-"Brause" 1337-Mate will welcome you at their site. After tasting their awakening Mate you'll enjoy delicious food in a local restaurant before we head on for cocktails at chillaxing 3 Freunde Bar. (Costs: ordered food & cocktails)

Hotels

For all of you who need a place to sleep in Hamburg during The Preface here are some easy options all in walking distance to the betahaus.

St. Pauli Lodge

Located no more than 4 minutes away from the venue (Map) the St. Pauli Lodge offers double and single rooms at a good rate including coffee, tea and wifi. Check their website for all the details

www.sankt-pauli-lodge.de (de)

MyPlace

For those who are looking to stay two nights in Hamburg the MyPlace is a good option. Again you won't walk for more than 5 minutes to the betahaus (Map). More information on the website

www.myplace-hamburg.de

bedpark

If you are on a budget the bedpark has you covered. It takes you a 10 minute walk to the betahaus (Map). Book rooms and get more details on their website: www.bedpark.de (de)
